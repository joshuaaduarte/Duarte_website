See ya later SHPE

Posted on June 15th, 2022

Today was the day that I have been dreading for many months now. The day that the NCP report is due and my last responsibility that correlates to TAMU SHPE.

TAMU SHPE has been the organization that changed my life over the course of my undergraduate career at A&M. The people that I met, the memories that I made, and impact I have been able to make on people is something that I will forever be in debt to TAMU SHPE for.

I spent ALL day working on the report. Finding the right phrasing of the accomplishments we made over the semester was pretty time consuming.

Ultimately, the team was able to review the report and found it suitable for submission. Elizabeth, my right hand in SHPE, spent her afternoon reviewing the work and ultimately joined me in submitting the report.

The Final Review

With this report submission, my role within TAMU SHPE has finally transitioned to former student. I hope to help those that go through the organization, and I can't wait to hear about how the chapter will do this year.

Again, it still does not feel real yet, so I will wait to talk more about SHPE for when it does.

Habit Tracking

Today, I journaled, did not work out, read, tracked my caloric intake, and did not work on at least one of my personal projects. Today was a still a good day.

What I am thankful for

I am thankful for SHPE. Thank you for everything.
