Good To Be Home

Posted on June 12th, 2022

I started my day by visiting the new high schools soccer fields. Soccer was a huge part of my life growing up. Especially during my high school years.

When I tell you I lost all things related to stamina I mean it. This semester and summer I have spent a lot of time working out, but more on the side of weight lifting as opposed to cardio. It showed today. Within the first few minutes I was covered in sweat and breathing a little heavier than normal.

When I make it back to College Station, I will most definetely playing more soccer. It was really great being able to kick around a ball like the old days.
