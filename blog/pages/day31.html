Posted on June 25th, 2022

The sign language trainer is one step closer to what I am trying to achieve. I have been able to get the program to recognize a few letter gestures and I hope to be even closer tomorrow.

I also got to finish another audiobook. I hope I will continue to finish books as the days progress and make it a habit that I will not struggle to keep.

Sign Language Trainer

I was able to be able to recognize hand gestures in the form of the letters A-L with the exception of 'J'. The reason I was not able to do 'J' is because it would require the program to understand movement as opposed to a set position. Once I have refined all the letters in the coming days, I will give both the letter J and Z some time to be able to recognize them.
