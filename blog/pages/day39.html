Do. Fail. Learn.

Posted on July 3rd, 2022

Build

From listening to the podcast that had Tony Fadell yesterday, I was inpsired to start reading his book. From the first few chapters I was able to gloss over, the "Do. Fail. Learn." quote stuck out to me. It has been something that I have always tried to do but never really put to words. I hope to always take this path with failures occurring less and less as I learn more. But the more you learn, the more you don't know.

Build by Tony Fadell

I hope to uncover some greater insights into what has shaped Tony throughout his career. I am sure it will impact me and those I will work with in the years to come.

Runnin'

I have never been the most avid runner. Sure I run while playing soccer, but I have never truly enjoyed running. At least for times longer than a few weeks. Today I took up my first guided run using the nike run club app. I have used the app before but never have I had a guided run. It was recommended to me by my good friend Kiana, and I greatly enjoyed it. I hope to become the running lover I have always hoped to be.

Nike Run Club

Fuego

The fuego steak taco at Fuego was one of the first meals I had in College Station. It was one of my favorite go to meals throughout my undergraduate years too. Going here for dinner made me reminisce on the memories I have had here and those that made my time at Aggieland so special.

The Home of the Fuego Steak Taco

Habit Tracking

Today, I journaled, worked out, read, tracked my caloric intake, and worked on at least one of my personal projects. Today was a good day.

What I am thankful for

I am thankful for those that have impacted my journey of learning. I have and will continue to be forever grateful for you.
