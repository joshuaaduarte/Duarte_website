Good To Be Home

Posted on June 13th, 2022

The National Chapter Program Report for SHPE is due in two days. I was able to set some time aside today to work on it in hopes of getting a lot completed. However, I got sucked into watching netflix which did not make me nearly as productive as I hoped I could have been.

The segment I anticipate struggling the most within this report is the chapter-by-the-numbers section. Here, I am expected to break down the semester into metrics that would appeal to the national review committee.

Within the next few couple of days, I hope to have most of the document finalized to where the rest of the executive board team could review everything. I am ready and not ready at the same time to get this report over with.

Habit Tracking

Today, I journaled, did not work out, read, did not track my caloric intake, and did not work on at least one of my personal projects. Today was a still a good day.

What I am thankful for

I am thankful for the SHPE executive board team. They were the best team I had the pleasure to work alongside and am forever thankful for them.
