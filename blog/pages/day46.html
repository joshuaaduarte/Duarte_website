With the sensing mechanism complete and tested, the only aspect left was to create the cutout within the acrylic top plate. Being that I am away from my desktop at the moment, I feared that I did not have the template model that I have used to solder the matrix together.

Although small, this small change resulted in my model no longer being accurate. I hope to create the new side panels to match the new dimensions in the days to come.
