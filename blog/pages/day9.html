My New Toy

3D printers have always fascinated me. Having some raw material turned into anything your imagination can fathom is something that I know will shape our future in the years to come. Today I was able to pick up my second 3D printer. This one however, uses a PLA plastic instead of SLA. For those that are not familiar, SLA printers use resin that when exposed to a specific wavelength of light, it hardens. Using a LCD that emits the specific wavelength, the printer is able create an object in a layer by layer format. The new printer I got uses a PLA plastic that has a relatively low melting temperature. With this a 3 dimensional gantry system is able to heat and extrude the plastic in a specif manner layer by layer until it is completed.

Ender 3 Pro 3

Although I loved my first printer, this new printer has a larger printing capacity to where I can print much larger things. I can't wait to see what comes out of it.
