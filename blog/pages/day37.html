July...Already?

Posted on July 1st, 2022

It's July 1st. Every time I take a good look at the calendar, I feel that the days are quickly passing me by. It is bittersweet. I know the future that awaits me will be nothing short of amazing, but I have greatly enjoyed this summer and the memories I have made throughout it. Not too sure if I am ready for it to end.

Gambit

A major component of Gambit is the electromagnet. It is responsible for moving the pieces on command. With that in mind, it has to be able to turn on and off on command. I spent some time figuring out how to do so.

I spent more time than I would like to admit in understanding how to accurately wire the system. It wasn't until I found this schematic that I was able to do so.

Circuit Schematic

This schematic allowed me to quickly create a script that allowed for the magnet to turn on and off on command.

Electromagnet Wiring
