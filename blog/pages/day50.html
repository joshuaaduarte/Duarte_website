How I've Missed You

Posted on July 14th, 2022

Seattle

Last summer, I worked in Seattle for my internship with Boeing. It was one of the better summers I have experienced. Not because of the work, but because of the city. I lived with Joanthan and got to experience the amazing weather, food, and people that made up this environment.

I started off the morning by getting some work done on Jonathans' rooftop. The views were spectacular, and will definetely be coming here again before we leave back to Texas.

Previous Next
The Rooftop

We also made it out to Pike Place market to check out some of the killer views. We came a little late which made it a little less busy than normal, but exciting nonetheless.

Pike Place Market

Our night ended after grabbing a meal from a favorite restaurant of mine and visiting Gas Works Park. Seeing the city from here is something that someone could never get tired of.

Gas Works Park

Eats

Un Bien is probably my favorite go to meal within Seattle. The sandwiches they make here are to die for. Surprisingly, the cashier recognized Joanthan and I which made us question how often Jonathan and I came.

Un Bien

Habit Tracking

Today, I journaled, did not work out, read, tracked my caloric intake, and worked on at least one of my personal projects. Today was a good day.

What I am thankful for

I am thankful for Jonathan, Elizabeth, and Jenny. I truly hope our friendship stays true regardless of distance as these next few years go by.
