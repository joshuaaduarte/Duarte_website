'Til We Meet Again

Posted on July 18th, 2022

Seattle -> Texas

Today was the last day of the amazing trip that I had to Seattle with Elizabeth and Jenny in visiting Jonathan. It was honestly great being around them again and I am definetely going to miss them in this journey that awaits me but I am positive our paths will cross again.

Our day started at 4:00 am where we began to prepare and make our way to the airport to catch our 6:00 am flight.

The Last Ride

We reflected a bit on our trip and were eager to see eachother again in the months to come.

Anxious Driver

In boarding the plane, it was almost perfect that the weather was gloomy as I imagine it reflected all of our emotions.

So Long

I also got the window seat which let me get a greater look at the world and environment that surrounds us. It was absolutely stunning and hope that I get to climb some mountains that are similar to the ones I saw sometime in my lifetime.

Some Mountain

After the long hours of flying, I was able to spend some time with my family before I made the commute back to College Station to continue working for the few days I have left on this 60 day journey.

Habit Tracking

Today, I journaled, did not work out, read, tracked my caloric intake, and worked on at least one of my personal projects. Today was a good day.

What I am thankful for

I am thankful yet again for Jonathan, Jenny, and Elizabeth as they have drastically shaped my life more than they could ever know.
