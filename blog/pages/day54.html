'Til We Meet Again

Posted on July 18th, 2022

Seattle

It was a bittersweet day knowing that this was the last day that we were all going to be together in Seattle. This would also be the last time we all were going to be together for the foreseable future. Nevertheless, we made the most of the day.

We made it out again to grab some food then out to one of the Amazon buildings. This time we were at Frontier where we had access to more killer views and free coffee. I am interested in seeing how the city will transform in the years to come as it becomes a hub of tech.

Amazon Views

Our dinner was filled with more amazing meals as I was able to snag a avocado salmon burger as everyone else had some great pasta. I will surely be regretting everything I ate once I make it back and start working out again. But that's a future me problem.

Salmon Burger

The night ended yet again with some poker. I was able to scrape a win by the skin of my teeth. I couldn't imagine playing with higher stakes.

Poker

Habit Tracking

Today, I journaled, did not work out, read, tracked my caloric intake, and worked on at least one of my personal projects. Today was a good day.

What I am thankful for

I am thankful for Jonathan in allowing us to crash at his place and enjoy a few days with him and his new home. I can't wait to see what this guy accomplishes in his career.
