I have a few days remaining in this journey and the idea of that is what kept me motivated to continue working throughout the day. I am sure these next few days will be extremely productive and am eager to see what comes out of them.

Gambit

While I was in Seattle, I was able to finally get the acrylic top routed which allows me to insert the wires and sensing mechanism for the board. I made it out today to create a more finished feel of the board.

Adding in the supports and more firmly attaching the walls of the board was really great. The acrylic fit like a glove.

On top of just adding the acrylic top, I went ahead and created a script that allowed me to insert a coordinate which then moved the electromagnet to said coordinate. It was crazy to see the gantry system work as well as it did.
