The End is Near

Posted on July 22nd, 2022

Gambit

I finally moved Gambit from the FEDC. It was bittersweet seeing the accumulation of work from the past year being put on a cart to be rolled away to my car. But before moving it, I was able to get some work in on it.

I began by adding some wires to the matrix to be more accessible at a later date. I then went on to write a script that would be capable of taking in two seperate coordinates from the user and bring the piece from one location to another with the electromagnet.

Board Wired Up

It was cool seeing the board in a better state with a chess piece actually on the board. It still has a while to go before it will be ready to play, but its a nice start.
