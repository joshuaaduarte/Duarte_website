Family Time

Posted on June 4th, 2022

Today was another day of being able to spend time with those that matter to me. I become more and more grateful for those I have in my life because of days like these.

Family

My day revolved around Centerville, Texas. Definetely bigger than what I imagined. The day was spent someone who is very important to me, her family, and mine. We gathered around a delicious home cooked meal and continuous stories of Guatemala. Loved every second of it.

Along with this, my amazing mother made me enough home cooked meals to last me the next ten days. I beyond grateful for her and grateful to be the only boy of a family of four. It comes with its perks.

Guess I don't need groceries

Trust that the fridge has been reorganized since writing this. Nevertheless, after the long day I got to enjoy a nice movie on the couch. Something I haven't been able to do in quite some time.

Nothing Like the Queen's Gambit

Habit Tracking

Today, I did not do a lot of my normal habits but it was still a good day.

What I am thankful for

I am thankful for my family and those that will turn into my family.

Enjoy your family and those you care about while you can. - J.D.
