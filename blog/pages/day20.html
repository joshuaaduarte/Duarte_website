Back to the Stat

Posted on June 14th, 2022

I was up bright and early today to get some work on the NCP report before I made my way back to College Station.

Last night, I said my goodbyes to the family members that I knew would not be awake by the time I left.

Before heading out, my mom packed some meals that should last me the next few days. It has been a while since I have gone grocery shopping and that's always nice.

I don't know when I will make it back out to Waxahachie, but it was really nice being able to see my family.

Once I made it back to College Station, I spent a good amount of time cleaning and once again working on the NCP report. It is looking like tomorrow will be a day full of working on the report. I truly hope it doesn't take as much time as I think it will.

Habit Tracking

Today, I journaled, did not work out, read, did not track my caloric intake, and did not work on at least one of my personal projects. Today was a still a good day.

What I am thankful for

I am thankful for Waxahachie. It is home to my family and a lot of great friends and memories that I miss dearly.
